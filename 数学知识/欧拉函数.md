# 理论基础
* 算数基本定理

$$
X = \prod_{i=1}^{n}{p_i^{a_i}} = p_1^{a_1}\*p_2^{a_2}\*...\*p_n^{a_n}
$$

* 欧拉函数

$$
\varphi(N) = N \* (1-\frac{1}{p_1}) \* (1-\frac{1}{p_2}) \*...\* (1-\frac{1}{p_n})
$$

* 欧拉定理

$$
gcd(a, m) = 1 \Rightarrow a^{\phi(m)} \equiv 1 (mod\ m)
$$

* 费马小定理

$$
p为素数，gcd(a, p) = 1 \Rightarrow a^{p-1} \equiv 1 (mod\ m)
$$
